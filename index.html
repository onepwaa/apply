About OnePwaa

OnePwaa is an inclusive, not-for-profit World Scholar's Cup community project bringing scholars together with free subject notes, blogs about our experiences, and memes! With almost 30 members from around the world, we are committed to developing collaborative learning experiences that focus on the process of learning itself instead of quantifying unique stories. This is why our motto is "the sound many happy alpacas make together"; we are invested in balance in education for the new world generation. FAQs

Can I apply to more than one team?

Yes, you can apply to a maximum of 2 teams. Should you be chosen, however, you will only be inducted into one team.

What’s required to be a OnePwaa member?

As a general rule, you only need to be a current scholar in WSC. This can slightly differ for each of the positions in our team, so be sure to check with the descriptions above.

My question hasn’t been answered. What do I do?
